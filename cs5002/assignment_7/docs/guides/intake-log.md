# Intake Log

The Intake Log tracks what you eat each day so you can monitor your calories and macronutrients (protein, carbs, fat) against your daily goals. The intake log is embedded directly into the [Home Dashboard](home.md).

## Logging Food

1. Go to the **Home** tab.
2. In the **Intake Log** card, tap **+ Log Unplanned Food** (or use the **Log Food** quick action button).
3. The **Add Food Sheet** opens — search for a food, recipe, or ingredient.
4. Select the item and set the quantity (number of servings).
5. Tap **Add** to log it.

The calorie ring and macro progress bars update immediately.

## Viewing Logged Items

All items logged for the selected day appear in the Intake Log section. Each entry shows:

- Item name
- Calorie and macro contribution

Tap any logged item to open its detail modal (either a **Meal Detail** or **Recipe Detail** view).

## Navigating Between Days

Use the **date navigator** strip at the top of the Home Dashboard to select a different day. Each day shows its total calorie count beneath the date. Arrow buttons on either side let you scroll to past or future dates.

## Editing or Removing Log Entries

- Tap a logged item to view its details.
- From the detail view, you can adjust the quantity or remove the entry.

## Understanding the Calorie Ring

The circular chart in the Intake Log shows:

- **Center number** — total calories consumed today
- **Ring progress** — percentage of your daily calorie goal reached
- **Below the ring** — "X items logged" and the percentage of your daily goal

The ring turns from gray to green as you approach your target.

## Macro Progress Bars

Three horizontal bars track your macronutrient intake:

| Macro | Icon | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Protein** | Flame | 0g / 150g |
| **Carbs** | Wheat | 0g / 250g |
| **Fat** | Droplet | 0g / 65g |

These targets come from your [Nutrition Goals](profile.md) in the Profile screen.

## Tips for Accuracy

- Use grams (g) for the most precise tracking when possible.
- If calorie totals seem off, double-check serving sizes and units.
- Log meals as soon as you eat them so you don't forget.
