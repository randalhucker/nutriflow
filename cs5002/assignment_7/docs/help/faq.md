# Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?
Open NutriFlow and click **Get Started** or **Sign Up**. You can register with an email and password, or use **Continue with Google** for one-click sign-up. See: [Quickstart](../getting-started/quickstart.md)

### Can I try NutriFlow without creating an account?
Yes. On the Sign In page, use the **Demo user** or **Premium demo** buttons for instant access with a pre-configured demo account.

### What is the difference between Free and Premium?
Free users have full access to meal planning, recipes, pantry, cart, intake logging, and insights. Premium adds **Household collaboration** (shared meal plans, pantry, and cart with family or roommates), receipt uploads, and advanced features. See: [Key Concepts](../reference/concepts.md)

## Recipes & Meal Planning

### Where do recipes come from?
NutriFlow has two recipe sources:
- **My Recipes** — recipes you create yourself
- **Discover** — a large catalog from Spoonacular, a third-party recipe database

### Can I filter recipes by what I already have?
Yes. On the Recipes page, toggle the **From My Pantry** filter to show only recipes whose ingredients match items in your pantry. See: [Recipes](../guides/recipes.md)

### How do I add a recipe to my meal plan?
Open any recipe and tap **Add to Meal Plan**. A slot picker lets you choose the day and meal slot (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack). See: [Meal Plans](../guides/meal-plans.md)

### Can I reuse a meal plan from a previous week?
Yes. Save your current plan as a template, then apply it to a future week from the Saved Plans panel. See: [Meal Plans](../guides/meal-plans.md)

## Nutrition Tracking

### Why don't my calories match my macros?
Common causes:
- **Serving size mismatch** — the food entry may use a different serving size than what you ate
- **Rounding** — nutrition labels round values, which can cause small discrepancies
- **Incomplete data** — some recipe or food entries may have approximate nutritional values

### How do I change my daily calorie and macro goals?
Go to **Profile** and tap the pencil icon on the **Nutrition Goals** card. Edit your daily calories, protein, carbs, and fat targets, then save. See: [Profile & Goals](../guides/profile.md)

### What is the calorie ring on the Home Dashboard?
The circular chart shows how many calories you have consumed today versus your daily goal. As you log food, the ring fills up from gray to green. See: [Home Dashboard](../guides/home.md)

## Shopping & Kroger

### How do I connect my Kroger account?
Go to **Profile**, find the **Kroger** card, and tap **Connect**. You will be redirected to Kroger's authorization page. Once approved, NutriFlow can pull real product names, prices, and images. See: [Profile & Goals](../guides/profile.md)

### How does NutriFlow generate a shopping cart?
NutriFlow reads the recipes in your active meal plan, calculates the required ingredients, subtracts items already in your pantry, and maps the remaining needs to Kroger products. Tap **Regenerate** on the Shopping page to build or refresh the cart. See: [Shopping & Cart](../guides/shopping.md)

### Can I manually add items to my cart?
Yes. On the Shopping page, tap **+ Add Item** to search the Kroger catalog and add any product.

## Pantry

### How do I add items to my pantry after shopping?
You have several options: tap **+ Add Item** to add manually, use **Add from Cart** to import purchased items from your shopping cart, or use **Add from Receipt** / **Add from Picture** for assisted entry. See: [Pantry](../guides/pantry.md)

### What do the expiration color badges mean?
- **Green** — fresh, plenty of time remaining
- **Yellow** — expiring soon (within a few days)
- **Red** — already expired
- **Gray** — no expiration date set

## Households

### Can I share meal plans with my family?
Yes, if you have a **Premium** account. Create or join a Household, then share meal plans from the Household page. Other members can view and apply shared plans. See: [Households](../guides/households.md)

### Can multiple people edit the cart and pantry?
Yes. Household admins control per-member permissions:
- **Cart: Edit** — allows adding, removing, and modifying cart items
- **Pantry: Edit** — allows adding, removing, and modifying pantry items

### How do I invite someone to my household?
Go to **Profile > Household** and tap **Invite Member**. Enter the person's email or share your household ID.

## Account & Settings

### How do I switch between light and dark mode?
Go to **Profile** and find the **Appearance** card at the bottom. Tap the **Dark** / **Light** button to toggle.

### How do I sign out?
Go to **Profile** and tap **Sign Out** at the bottom of the page. A confirmation dialog appears before you are logged out.

### How do I set my dietary preferences?
Go to **Profile > Dietary Preferences** to set your diet type, allergens, and food category interests. See: [Dietary Preferences](../guides/dietary-preferences.md)
