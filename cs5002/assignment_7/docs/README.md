# NutriFlow User Guide & Manual

Welcome to **NutriFlow**, a nutrition-forward meal planning, grocery shopping, and intake tracking application. NutriFlow helps you:

- **Plan meals** with a visual weekly calendar and a library of recipes
- **Track nutrition** by logging daily food intake against calorie and macro goals
- **Manage your pantry** to reduce food waste and shop smarter
- **Build smart shopping carts** powered by Kroger store integration
- **Collaborate with a household** to share meal plans, pantry, and carts (Premium)
- **Monitor progress** with insights dashboards, streaks, and spending analytics
