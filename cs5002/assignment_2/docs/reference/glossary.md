# Glossary

**Food**: A user-defined nutrition entry.  
**Ingredient**: A global/scraped nutrition entry.  
**Meal**: A reusable combination of foods/ingredients/recipes.  
**Recipe**: Instructions + ingredient list + servings.  
**Meal Plan**: A schedule of meals across dates/times.  
**Pantry**: Inventory of what you have at home.  
**Cart**: Grocery list for stores.  
**Household**: A shared group for planning and shopping.  
**Visibility**: Who can see an item (private/household/public).
