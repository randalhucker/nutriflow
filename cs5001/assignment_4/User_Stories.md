# NutriFlow - User Stories (Assignment #4)

- As a home cook, I want to build a weekly **meal plan** from recipes and foods so that I can hit my nutrition targets without overthinking each day.
  - As a home cook, I want NutriFlow to suggest recipes based on what’s in my pantry and my nutrition goals so that I can quickly decide what to cook without starting from scratch.
- As a grocery shopper, I want NutriFlow to auto-create my **cart** from my **meal plan**, taking my **pantry** contents into account, so that I spend less time shopping and avoid buying duplicates.
  - As a grocery shopper, I want to snap a photo of a grocery receipt and have NutriFlow update my pantry automatically so that I don’t have to enter every item by hand.
  - As an online grocery shopper, I want NutriFlow to send my finalized cart to my preferred store’s website so that I can check out with minimal extra steps.
- As a member of my household, I want to manage our shared **pantry** so that we know what we have, avoid food waste, and keep everyone on the same page.
- As a user with dietary restrictions, I want to filter and substitute ingredients in my **meal plan** based on my **allergens and preferences** so that I can cook safely and still enjoy variety.
- As a fitness-focused user, I want to log what I eat and see macro roll-ups against **daily targets** so that I can adjust portions and stay on track to meet my goals.
- As an aspiring foodie, I want to **explore recipes and meal suggestions** based on my **diet** and **preferences** so that I can broaden my horizons and find new favorites.
- As a budget-focused user, I want NutriFlow to compare prices for my cart across nearby stores so that I can choose where to shop based on total cost.
- As a privacy-conscious user, I want to control what purchase and nutrition data NutriFlow stores and how it’s used so that I can benefit from recommendations without sharing more than I’m comfortable with.
